About Me

Royal Marines Commando turned Software developer. I've spent the past nine years involved in military operations aross the globe, including the defence of UK nuclear assets, providing humanitarian aid during the European migrant crisis and interdicting maritime drug smuggling vessels.

More recently, I have trained as a Communication Systems Engineer in order to provide satellite communications for 3 Commando Brigade. Alongside a BSc degree in computing from The Open University, my occupational experience has given me a firm grasp of networking and programming fundamentals across a wide range of high tempo environments.

My goal over the next few years is to grow my skills in as many programming languages as possible, with the ultimate aim of becoming a full time developer.
